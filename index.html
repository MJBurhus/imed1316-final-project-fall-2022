Volunteer Opportunities

Latte Libros is about making a difference in our community. What better way to make a difference than to open up the worlds of wonder that can be found in books?

So many members of our community are prevented from entering these worlds for many reasons. Some are too young to read, some struggle with learning to read, and some have lost the ability to read. Nothing is more powerful or important for these friends than listening to someone read.

Volunteering as a Latte Libros Reading Buddy is not just another resposibility, it is an adventure, an enjoyable opportunity to assist and commune with other people of all ages.

Contact Our Bookstore

It is the desire of our Latte Libros community to share experiences of our different histories, cultures, folklore, and foods. To this end, Latte Libros carries books from many countries and ethnic authors including biographies and autobiographies in-store and online. Available to customers are the common classifications including literary fiction, science fiction, horror, romance, mystery, fantasy, poetry, drama, humor, history, photography, art, travel, and do-it-your-self books and magazines.

However, Latte Libros is more than a bookstore, it is a place to gather and share. Our store provides comfortable seating and rooms for private meetings, children's activities, games or study. Multiple caffeinated and non-caffeinated beverages, foods and treats are available for purchase at our cafe, The Daily Espresso.

Whether you are looking for a special book, a place to meet, an activity to engage in, or good food and great company, we are waiting to serve you. Feel free to contact us during business hours and anytime online.
